About the FreeBSD Graphics Team

The FreeBSD Graphics Team is responsible for all components of the graphics stack on FreeBSD. This includes video and input devices kernel drivers, the X.Org server, Mesa, Wayland and a bunch of applications and libraries around them.

Team members

The main members are:

  • Koop Mast
  • Jean-Sébastien Pédron

How to reach us

We can be reached through:

  • the freebsd-x11@FreeBSD.org mailing-list;
  • the #freebsd-xorg IRC channel on EFnet.

We also publish what we work on on the FreeBSD wiki graphics portal.
